# iDea
 An Information DissEmination Application that runs on Mountain Top University's (MTU's) intranet thereby allowing for secure and controlled communication within the school community.
## Statement of Problem (MTU AS A CASE STUDY)
<p>It was discovered that the amount of wrong information been passed around in the university was too much which leads to missed assessment work, waste of the time of the lecturers and students, missed classes, and even issues with clashing courses. This study is here to solve said problems to avoid future headache for both the management and the students across the university.
</p>
<p><b>For Example:</b></p>
<ul>
 <li>A lecturer has to call the hostel porters if the attention of a student is needed in the college area    student regardless of if he or she is in the hostel. 
</li>

</ul>

## Aim and Objectives
<ol>
 <li>Creating an intranet-based information dissemination system to facilitate communication and learning in a school where phones are not permitted.
</li>
 <li>Developing a system with intuitive user interfaces and a variety of communication channels to make it easy and comfortable for teachers and students to utilize.
</li>
 <li>Evaluating the information distribution system's performance in increasing communication and boosting student learning, as well as comparing its efficiency and accuracy to earlier communication techniques.
</li>
</ol>
